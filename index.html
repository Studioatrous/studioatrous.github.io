The Philosophy

A cross-cultural
perspective on modern living.

Studio Atrous is a boutique design practice born from a passion for spaces that breathe. We serve a discerning clientele in Galway, Ireland, and across India, focusing on residential layouts that balance architectural rigor with human warmth.

Our approach is intentional. Whether it's a coastal home on the Wild Atlantic Way or a sprawling residence in India, we prioritize functional luxury—ensuring every texture, light source, and layout serves a purpose.

We specialize in the "Unseen Luxury"—the way a room flows, the storage that hides the clutter, and the materials that age gracefully over decades.
